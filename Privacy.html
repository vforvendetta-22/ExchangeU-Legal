Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 25, 2025

ExchangeU values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information we collect
- Account Information: Email address (.edu), name, and password.
- Listings & Content: Photos, item descriptions, and messages you post.
- Usage data: Interactions within the app (analytics, crash reports).
- Device Data: Device type, operating system, and identifiers.

2. How we use information
- To provide and improve the ExchangeU service.
- To verify student status and maintain a safe, student-only marketplace.
- To monitor app performance and fix bugs (via Firebase).
- To enforce our Terms of Service and protect the community.

3. Sharing of Information
We do not sell your data. We may share limited data:
- With trusted third-party service providers (e.g., Firebase for hosting, analytics, and authentication).
- To comply with legal requests or to protect safety.
- During a business transfer (e.g., merger, acquisition).

4. Data Retention
- We keep your data as long as you have an account.
- You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

5. Your Rights
Depending on your location, you may have rights to:
- Access and update your information.
- Request deletion of your information.
- Opt out of certain data processing.

6. Security
We use reasonable measures (including Firebase security rules and encryption in transit) to protect your data. No system is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy
ExchangeU is intended only for college students with .edu emails. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email.
